About Us

HighRVA’s mission is to promote and provide natural cannabis solutions that enhance the health and well-being of registered patients in a safe, open and caring environment designed and dedicated to respecting the rights of the individual and our community. We welcome, value and rely on the diversity of people, cultural experiences and perspectives. We learn from one another. Through our daily interactions with patients we refine (and sometimes redesign) solutions that promote a better dispensary experience and elevate our patients’ physical, emotional and psychological wellness. We are committed to making access to cannabis products and information easy and reliable for everyone.
